Features

Our software is state of the art and covers all the functionality required to do a safe and timely turnover. If you encounter a situation where our software doesn't quite fit your needs we'll be happy to work with you to find a solution. Our developers can quickly add or alter functionality to meet with your requirements.

Our current feature set includes:

  • Scoping of tags into systems and subsystems
  • Scoping of tags into areas
  • Tablet based completions
  • Barcoded check sheet generation(for sites unsitable for tablets)
  • Preservation management with worklist generation and delinquiency reporting
  • Hydrotest package management
  • Progress reporting by system and subsystem
  • Progress reporting by area
  • Progress reporting by CWP/EWP
  • Punch item tracking
  • Full audit log to see what changed and when
